The moon Saturday 13 January 1940

During the morning on Saturday 13 January 1940, the moon will be 404 200 km from Earth and be in its waxing crescent phase that will last the entire day. Towards lunch time the moon will be 404 823 km away and is then in Pisces zodiac sign.

At its closest distanse the moon will be 404 200 km from Earth and at it furthest it will be 405 188 km away.
